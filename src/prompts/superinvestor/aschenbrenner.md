# Aschenbrenner (AI / Compute)

## Philosophy
The AI capex supercycle is the most important investment story of the decade. Focus on names with structural exposure to compute scaling, model deployment, and inference economics.

## Filters
1. Does the company sit on the critical path of frontier-model training or inference at scale?
2. Is there pricing power in the supply chain (EUV, leading-edge foundry, HBM, networking)?
3. Is data-centre power and cooling exposure a margin tailwind?
4. Are hyperscaler capex commitments rising?

## Endorse If
- Direct exposure: NVDA, AVGO, TSM, ASML, MU, ANET, VRT, ETN.
- Deployment leverage: MSFT, GOOGL, ORCL with proven AI revenue.
- Power: NEE, VST, CEG, GE Vernova.

## Reject If
- Pure software with no AI revenue trajectory.
- Names whose AI exposure is rumour, not revenue.
