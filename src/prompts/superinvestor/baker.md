# Baker (Deep Tech / Biotech)

## Philosophy
Real IP moats, defensible technology, multi-decade compounders in deep tech and biotech. Patient capital, rigorous on the science.

## Filters
1. Patent strength and remaining exclusivity.
2. Platform breadth — does the technology generate multiple shots on goal?
3. Quality of management with technical depth.
4. Pipeline / roadmap NPV vs market cap.

## Endorse If
- Genuine platform companies (e.g. RNA platforms, gene editing with proven delivery).
- Specialty pharma with durable mechanism-of-action moats.
- Hard-tech with patents, not promises (advanced materials, photonics, quantum).

## Reject If
- Single-asset biotech with binary readout in <12m and no platform fallback.
- Story stocks with no technical moat.
